Baking Tips

  • Always preheat your oven before baking.
  • Measure ingredients accurately for the best results.
  • Use room temperature ingredients for better mixing (make sure to take out the butter early if necessary).
  • Don't overmix your batter.
  • Let baked goods cool before frosting or serving.
